He knew parts of his technique could be sharper, and he wanted mentorship and a community around him — not just information. So he eventually sought coaching. His best results came after being coached, which deepened his belief that the right guidance at the right time accelerates everything.

He started cold approach in September 2024 and got his first date in January 2025. He sought coaching between July and August 2025. The results that followed were transformative:

- Broke a four-year sexless dry streak within weeks of starting coaching
- His overall conviction and confidence skyrocketed — the quality of women he attracted improved noticeably
- Started meeting women and taking them out for drinks on the same day — something he'd never done before
- Made out with a girl in a coffee shop
- Hooked up with women at house parties purely through his newfound social confidence
- Dated three stunning women during a trip to Thailand in November 2025
- Met a beautiful 22-year-old French woman in October 2025 — a relationship he is still in today

All of this without dating apps, looksmaxxing, or any change in income or status. Simply by learning to meet women in person and doing the inner work to back it up.

**What makes Ian's story credible and differentiated:**
